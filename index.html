ABOUT US

A COMPANY BY RUNNERS

A company started by long time member of Hyderabad Runners. Company consists of all runners who have been running marathons.
Committed to all events specially events organized for a cause. We use the latest 2019 manufactured MYLAPS timing system, the oldest
and most widely used chip timing system in the world. MYLAPS is infact the first company in the world to envisage and employ RFID
technology to time running and other such events. The accuracy of the system is widely acknowledge and is often used in all the major
events of the world,including the Sochi OLYMPICS. MYLAPS timing system relegates the inherent weaknesses of conventional timing
methods and effectively addresses problems like slow starts caused by crowded starting areas, multiple-chute finishing areas, cheating
and other such issues associated with earlier timing systems. It also helps in recording accurate split times and provides instant race updates.
